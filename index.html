Our Process

At Scribey, we believe that every individual is unique, and so are their journaling needs. That's why we have developed a personalized selection process that helps us match you with the perfect journal or notebook.

When you subscribe to Scribey, you will fill out a survey that asks about your preferences in terms of journal size, cover design, paper quality, and more. Our algorithm then uses this information to select a journal that matches your preferences.

But our process doesn't end there. We also take into account your feedback and ratings on the journals you receive, so that we can continue to refine our selection process and provide you with even better journals in the future.
